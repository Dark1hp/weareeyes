Care, use warranty, conformance and definitions for cleaning and maintenance

To help preserve the superior optical quality of your lenses, please use the cleaning cloth that is specially designed for cleaning your lenses . Hand wash and dry it regularly. Use only mild soap and water to clean your product. Never “dry rub” your polarized lenses, even with a clean microfiber cloth. Use water first especially if there is salt residue (from seawater) or any other type of abrasive material on your glasses.

Restrictions of use

This eyewear is not designed to protect the wearer from all injury in the event of impact with hard objects. To perform properly the lenses must be fitted securely in the frame. Unless marked and identified as such, sunglasses are not intended for use as industrial protection equipment. ‘’WEAREEYES’’ eyewear should not be used for direct viewing of the sun or strong artificial light sources and it is no protection against artificial light, nor should tinted lenses be used for driving at night with poor visibility, or dawn. If the eyewear impairs your peripheral vision, do not wear while driving .Certain lens tints are specifically designed for performance applications and may be unsuitable for traffic signal recognition.
